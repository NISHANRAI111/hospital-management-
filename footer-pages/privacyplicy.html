Privacy Policy

Important: This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information in the MNMN. Please read carefully.

Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how MNMN collects, uses, and protects personal information provided by users (patients, doctors, receptionists, and admins). By using the MNMN, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

Information Collection

We collect the following types of personal information:

  • Personal Data: Name, contact details, medical history, appointment requests, and other health-related information provided by patients.
  • System Usage Data: Information on user activity within the system, such as login attempts, appointment requests, and interactions with medical records.
  • Professional Data: For healthcare providers, we collect professional qualifications, specialties, and work schedules.
  • Administrative Data: For staff members, we collect employment details and system access logs.

Use of Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Facilitate and manage appointments, treatments, and medical reports.
  • Ensure secure user authentication and prevent unauthorized access.
  • Track patient billing, payments, and hospital service usage.
  • Improve the quality of services provided by analyzing system usage and user feedback.
  • Communicate with users regarding appointment status, billing, and hospital services.
  • Maintain accurate medical records for continuity of care.
  • Comply with legal and regulatory requirements.

Data Security

We employ a range of security measures to protect your personal data, including:

  • Encryption: All sensitive personal and medical data is encrypted during storage and transmission.
  • Access Control: Strict access controls to ensure only authorized personnel (such as doctors and admin staff) can access certain types of personal information.
  • Authentication: Secure login processes with multi-factor authentication options to prevent unauthorized access to the system.
  • Audit Logs: Comprehensive logging of all access to sensitive data for security monitoring.
  • Regular Security Assessments: Periodic testing of our security systems and protocols.

Data Sharing

We do not share your personal data with third parties unless required by law or with your explicit consent. However, the following parties may have access to certain information:

  • Healthcare Providers: Doctors and other healthcare professionals involved in your care.
  • Hospital Staff: Receptionists and admins for scheduling, billing, and administrative tasks.
  • Legal Authorities: When required by law or in response to valid legal processes.
  • Emergency Situations: When necessary to protect vital interests in emergency medical situations.

Any third-party service providers with access to data are contractually bound to maintain confidentiality and security.

Data Retention

Personal and medical data is retained:

  • For as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this policy
  • To comply with legal obligations and medical record retention requirements
  • To resolve disputes and enforce our agreements
  • In accordance with applicable laws and regulations

When data is no longer needed, it is securely deleted or anonymized.

Patient Rights

As a patient, you have the right to:

  • Access: Request a copy of your personal and medical records.
  • Correction: Request corrections to your personal information if it is inaccurate or incomplete.
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your personal data, subject to applicable laws and medical record retention requirements.
  • Restriction: Request limitations on how your data is used in certain circumstances.
  • Objection: Object to specific processing of your personal data.
  • Portability: Request transfer of your data to another provider where technically feasible.

To exercise these rights, please contact the hospital administration.

Cookies and Tracking Technologies

Our system may use cookies and other tracking technologies to:

  • Enhance the user experience and system functionality
  • Analyze system performance and usage patterns
  • Maintain secure sessions and prevent fraudulent activity
  • Remember user preferences and settings

You can manage cookie preferences through your browser settings, though disabling certain cookies may affect system functionality.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect:

  • Changes in our data practices
  • New legal or regulatory requirements
  • System enhancements or modifications

Users will be notified of significant changes through system notifications or email, and the updated policy will be available on the HMS platform. Continued use of the system after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
