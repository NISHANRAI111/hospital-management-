Advertising Policy

Important: This Advertising Policy outlines how advertisements are managed within the MNMN to ensure they align with our standards of quality, relevance, and user privacy.

Introduction

This Advertising Policy governs how advertisements are handled and displayed within the MNMN. The HMS is committed to maintaining the privacy and security of all users while ensuring any advertising content complies with relevant laws and guidelines.

Types of Advertisements

The MNMN provide the following types of advertisements:

  • Healthcare-related Ads: Advertisements for medical services, health products, pharmaceutical companies, and wellness programs that align with the purpose of the system.
  • Non-healthcare Ads: Limited and relevant ads such as insurance, health-related technology, and other services that may be useful for hospital staff and patients.
  • Sponsored Content: Content that is sponsored or endorsed by third parties but labeled as such to distinguish it from standard information.
  • Educational Content: Informational materials from accredited medical institutions or health organizations.
  • Hospital Services: Promotions for services offered directly by the hospital or affiliated providers.

Ad Placement Guidelines

All advertisements placed on the MNMN platform will:

  • Be clearly distinguishable from content related to hospital services and system functionality.
  • Not interfere with the user experience or disrupt navigation within the system.
  • Not promote any illegal activities, offensive content, or misleading information.
  • Adhere to all applicable advertising standards and regulations, including healthcare advertising guidelines.
  • Be relevant and useful to the target audience (patients, doctors, admins, and receptionists).
  • Include clear labeling to identify the content as advertising when appropriate.
  • Maintain appropriate frequency to avoid overwhelming users.

Ad Targeting

Advertising may be targeted based on the user's role and preferences. However, no personal or sensitive medical data will be used for targeting ads. The MNMN will ensure that:

  • Ads displayed to patients are healthcare-related or relevant to their medical needs.
  • Ads targeted to doctors, receptionists, and admins are aligned with their professional responsibilities and interests.
  • Targeting is based only on general user categories and never on specific medical conditions.
  • All targeted advertising complies with privacy regulations and ethical guidelines.
  • Users have visibility into why they are seeing particular advertisements.

User Consent

  • Users may be asked for consent to display ads, especially if targeting is involved.
  • Users can opt out of personalized ad targeting, but may still see generic or non-targeted ads.
  • Users' personal and medical data will never be shared with advertisers without explicit consent.
  • Consent preferences can be adjusted at any time through user account settings.
  • No advertising will be shown to users who have opted out of all non-essential communications.

Prohibited Ads

The following types of advertisements are strictly prohibited on the MNMN platform:

  • Advertisements for tobacco, alcohol, or illicit drugs.
  • Ads that promote harmful, misleading, or unapproved health practices or products.
  • Ads that are discriminatory, offensive, or violate the rights of individuals.
  • Any form of clickbait or deceptive advertising that misrepresents the advertised content.
  • Ads making unsubstantiated health claims or promising unrealistic results.
  • Political advertising or content unrelated to healthcare services.
  • Ads that could compromise patient care or professional medical judgment.

Third-Party Advertisers

The MNMN may use third-party advertising networks or partners to serve ads within the system. These third parties must comply with the same advertising standards as the MNMN and ensure that:

  • Ads do not compromise the security or privacy of users.
  • No personal or medical data is shared with third-party advertisers without proper consent.
  • Advertisements are compliant with all relevant laws and regulations governing healthcare advertising.
  • All third-party ads undergo the same review process as direct advertisements.
  • Contracts with third-party advertisers include strict data protection clauses.
  • Third parties are regularly audited for compliance with MNMN advertising standards.

Ad Reporting and Monitoring

  • The MNMN will actively monitor the content and performance of advertisements to ensure they adhere to this policy.
  • Users may report inappropriate or misleading ads to the admin team through a dedicated reporting mechanism.
  • Any ad that violates the policy will be removed promptly, typically within 24 hours of reporting.
  • The MNMN maintains a blacklist of advertisers who repeatedly violate advertising guidelines.
  • Regular audits will be conducted to assess ad quality and compliance.
  • Advertising performance metrics will be reviewed to ensure they meet user experience standards.

Changes to the Advertising Policy

  • This policy may be updated from time to time to reflect changes in advertising practices or regulations.
  • Any significant changes will be communicated to users through system notifications.
  • The updated policy will be accessible within the MNMN platform with clear version tracking.
  • Users are encouraged to review the policy periodically for updates.
  • Continued use of the MNMN after policy changes constitutes acceptance of the updated terms.
